The Art Showcase Website was the first iteration of the idea that later became
[Scroll.club](/projects/scroll-club). Users could upload artworks, follow other
artists, and get a personalised dashboard of the newest work from the people
they followed.

## Highlights

- Upload and manage a personal gallery of artworks
- Follow other artists and get a feed of their latest uploads
- Like and comment on any artwork
- Dashboard surfacing the newest work from followed artists

## Stack

Built with **Laravel** and **MySQL**, rendered server-side with HTML and CSS.
