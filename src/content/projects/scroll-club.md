Scroll.club is a members-only community platform where artists can build a
profile, publish their artwork, and follow each other. It grew out of the
earlier [Art Showcase Website](/projects/art-showcase) prototype.

## Highlights

- Invite-code registration so membership stays curated
- Artist profiles with galleries of uploaded work
- A social feed to follow artists and discover new pieces
- Likes and comments on individual artworks

## Stack

Built on **Laravel** with a **MySQL** database and a server-rendered
Blade/HTML/CSS frontend.
